Circle-Strafing

What is Circle-Strafing?

Circle-strafing is a movement technique used to evade attacks while continuously applying pressure to an enemy.

Steps

  1. Lock-On: Keep your aim focused on the enemy.
  2. Move in a Circle: Strafe around the enemy while keeping your crosshair on them.
  3. Maintain Momentum: Adjust your movement to avoid projectiles or attacks.

Pro Tips

  • Combine with dashes to add unpredictability.
  • Perfect for fights against stationary or slow-moving enemies.
  • Use alongside close-range weapons for maximum damage.
