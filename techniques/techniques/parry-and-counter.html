Parry and Counter

What is Parry and Counter?

Parry and Counter is a defensive technique that involves timing your attacks to deflect incoming projectiles or melee strikes, often followed by a counter-attack.

Steps

  1. Identify an Attack: Watch for a projectile or melee attack heading your way.
  2. Time Your Attack: Use the correct weapon or punch to parry the attack just before it connects.
  3. Follow Up: Immediately counter-attack to punish your opponent.

Pro Tips

  • Practice the timing for different enemy attacks.
  • Use the Whiplash to pull enemies closer before parrying their melee strikes.
  • Combine with Railcoin-Punching for high-damage counters.
