Railcoin-Punching

What is Railcoin-Punching?

Railcoin-Punching is an advanced technique that combines the Marksman revolver, punch redirection, and the railgun for devastating damage.

Steps

  1. Spawn a Coin: Use the Marksman revolver's alt-fire to create a coin.
  2. Punch the Coin: Punch the coin to direct it toward your target.
  3. Railgun Follow-Up: Immediately fire the railgun at the coin for a high-damage shot.

Pro Tips

  • Aim for weak spots to maximize damage.
  • Practice timing your punches to redirect coins more effectively.
  • Combine with movement techniques like strafing to stay evasive while executing the combo.
