Shotgun Boost

What is Shotgun Boost?

The Shotgun Boost is a mobility trick that uses the shotgun's alternate fire to propel yourself quickly in any direction, often for increased speed or to reach high places.

Steps

  1. Switch to Shotgun: Equip the Pump Shotgun.
  2. Load a Grenade: Use the alternate fire to load a grenade into the shotgun.
  3. Aim and Fire: Aim downward or in the desired direction and fire the grenade.

Pro Tips

  • Combine with dashes for longer boosts.
  • Use at an angle for diagonal movement.
  • Practice timing the boost to chain multiple movements fluidly.
