Weapon Swapping

What is Weapon Swapping?

Weapon Swapping is a technique where you quickly switch between weapons mid-combat to maximize damage output, exploit weaknesses, or maintain offensive pressure. This technique is key to mastering the high-speed gameplay of ULTRAKILL.

Steps to Perform Weapon Swapping

  1. Set Up Your Weapons: Equip weapons that complement each other (e.g., Nailgun and Railgun, Shotgun and Revolver).
  2. Assign Hotkeys: Use your weapon hotkeys or scroll wheel to switch weapons instantly.
  3. Attack, Swap, Repeat: Perform an attack, then immediately swap to another weapon and attack again. Repeat for a combo chain.

Examples of Weapon Swapping Combos

  • Shotgun to Revolver: Fire a Shotgun shell for close-range damage, then switch to the Revolver for a precise Railcoin shot.
  • Nailgun to Railgun: Use the Nailgun to build sustained DPS, then switch to the Railgun for a finishing blow.
  • Rocket Launcher to Shotgun: Launch a rocket at an enemy, swap to the Shotgun to hit them in close range during the explosion.

Advanced Tips

  • Practice Timing: Smoothly chain attacks to avoid wasting time during swaps.
  • Exploit Enemy Vulnerabilities: Swap weapons to match enemy weaknesses (e.g., Railgun for fast-moving targets).
  • Combine with Techniques: Pair weapon swaps with moves like Shotgun Boost or Railcoin-Punching for maximum efficiency.
  • Hotkey Preference: Bind frequently swapped weapons to accessible keys for faster execution.
